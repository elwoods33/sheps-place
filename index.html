Every dog deserves a home!

Shep's Place Senior Dog Sanctuary is a haven for older dogs who deserve a lifetime of love and care. Unlike shelters or rescues, our focus is on providing a permanent, loving home for these gentle souls. Whether they find a perfect match with a forever family, or spend their golden years with us, their happiness and well-being are our top priority.
